UHack 2017

University of Miami's Premier Hackathon

UHack is a 24 hour hackathon hosted by the University of Miami. It'll be held on the University of Miami campus ballroom in the Shalala Center. It is completely FREE for anyone in the area, and will be fully equipped with any snacks and drinks you may need to keep you going throughout the night.

Rocket Canes

The Rocket Canes are set to compete in the 2017 Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competion! We will achieve 30,000ft (cruising altitude of a 747!) after spending all year testing and designing the vehicle. Last year we achieved 3rd at the Hybrid Motor Rocket Competition. For more info, contact project manager, German Acosta Quiros at acqugerman@gmail.com. New members are always welcome!

Design Build Fly

Design Build Fly is an RC airplane competition where the airplane is - you guessed it - designed, built, and flown by the students. The team will be competing in the Tucson, Arizona on April 7th, 2017. For more info, contact the project manager, Michael Lennon at m.lennon2@miami.edu.

Astronomy Club

The Astronomy Club is a group within AIAA, aimed to observe the heavens. Activities include viewing sessions, trips to the planetarium, and learning all the basics of astrophysics. If interested, contact president Alexander Berne at acb150@miami.edu.
