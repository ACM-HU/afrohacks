Frequently Asked Questions


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a weekend long event where you go all the way from idea to prototype. At the end of AfroHacks, you will pitch your app, website, or whatever prototype you dreamed up, and compete for prizes! Hackathons are a great place to learn quickly and create something awesome.


How much does it cost?

AfroHacks is completely free for all registered participants! The food is free and all the awesome swag is free.


Will there be food?

Absolutely! We have four delicious meals planned with plenty of snacks in between to keep you going for 24 hours.


Is there any travel reimbursement?

Unfortunately, we will not be offering individual travel reimbursement this year.


Do you have a Code of Conduct?

Yes! Check out MLH's Code of Conduct .


What should I bring?

Bring you, your computer, computer charger, toiletries (toothbrush and toothpaste), and anything else for your hack.


This is my first hackathon!

You're more than welcome at AfroHacks! We will have workshops during the event and mentors to help you through any troubles. So no matter what your skill level is, we will provide the resources to help you learn.


How big are teams and what if I don’t have one?

Gather your friends, meet some peeps at opening, or attend our team-creation event right after opening ceremonies. No worries about knowing anyone beforehand. AfroHacks is for creating new connections and new people from around the states. Maximum 4 people per team.

Parking and Directions

Parking is free for the duration of the event at Pavia Garage, address 5616 Pavia Street, Coral Gables, FL on campus.
