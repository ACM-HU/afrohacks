FAQs


Can I attend this event?

Sure! If you are in the DMV and are at least 18 years of age you are welcome to attend.

How much does it cost?

AfroHacks is 100% free of charge to all registrants.

How do I get there?

Feel free to Uber into the area or take the Metro! If you decide to utilize the metro, be sure to get on the Green line and take it to the Shaw/Howard stop. Lewis K. Downing Hall is about a 10 minute walk from the station.

Can I bring a team?

You sure can. But please, limit your team sizes to groups of FOUR.

How do I join a team?

If you know some people that you would like to work with, figure out a cool name and make sure that you all list the same name in the Teams section of your registration application. Spelling is important.

What if I've never built an app before?

Don't worry! AfroHacks is an event designed to foster all aspects of technology. Even if you don't code, there are events designed for you. If you're a designer, entrepreneur, hardware specialist or anything inbetween there is a place for you at AfroHacks.

What will I eat?

We'll provide food for all meals from Friday's dinner to Saturday's dinner. If you have dietary restrictions we will try our best to accommodate them. Also there will be tons of snacks and drinks.

What should I bring?

Please bring a valid form of identification and a laptop. If you want to be comfortable we suggest also brining a blanket, pillows and slippers.

I have a question that wasn't answered here.

Please email us at afrohackshu@gmail.com. We'd love to help out!
